🚀 Getting Started & Registering

To track your listening statistics, you need to register an account by choosing a password and adding your Spotify session cookies:

  1. Go to the Register page.
  2. Input your email and choose a strong password.
  3. Locate your open.spotify.com session cookies from developer tools (F12) under application storage.
  4. Paste the cookies JSON or format block into the cookies field and click register.

Once registered, a dedicated background worker is initialized for your profile to automatically listen for track play updates.

🍪 Cookie-Based Real-time Sync

The primary ingestion method relies on your Spotify Web Player cookie. The background service uses this session token to mimic the active player connection:

  • Zero Premium Requirement: Works seamlessly for Free accounts without requiring subscription tokens.
  • Real-time Polling: Queries your player states every few seconds to record plays and duration stats.
  • Safety Guards: Includes timestamp checks to prevent cross-user contamination or invalid entries.

Note: If you log out of Spotify globally or your session expires, the sync state will shift to DEAD. Simply update your cookies in the Profile settings to resume.

🔑 Spotify Developer API Backfill

For resilience against cookie expiry or temporary downtime, you can configure Spotify Developer credentials:

  • Missed Plays Recovery: Scans the Spotify Web API recently-played history (up to 50 tracks) to fetch songs missed while offline.
  • Automatic Polling: Triggers backfill routines in the background every 15 minutes.
  • Duplicate Prevention: Uses timestamp-drift filters to avoid duplicating plays recorded via cookies.

Configure this in your Profile by pasting your Spotify Client ID and Client Secret, then authenticating via OAuth.
