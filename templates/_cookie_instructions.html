How to locate your session data

  1. Open open.spotify.com in a new browser tab and log in if not already logged in.
  2. Open Developer Tools (F12).
  3. Go to the Application tab (Chrome/Edge) or Storage tab (Firefox) and expand Cookies on the left.
  4. Click the https://open.spotify.com entry, then select and copy everything in that table.
  5. Paste it into the Cookies field above.
